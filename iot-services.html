Why is IoT used?

IoT helps make our lives simpler, safer, and more fun by linking devices, improving experiences, and offering useful information, which leads to a more connected and sustainable world.

What is a transformer monitoring system?

A system that monitors and examines the condition and performance of transformers as they operate, enabling the detection of issues, allowing for predictive maintenance, and improving the efficiency of transformers.

What is data center environmental monitoring?

Data center environmental monitoring is a process of constant observation of temperature, humidity, airflow, and many other factors with the help of sensors so that operations run smoothly without any issues in equipment and save energy.

What is a street light monitoring system?

The streetlight monitoring system integrates sensors, software, and tools to monitor streetlights remotely. This will enable instant identification of problems, better planning of repairs, and more efficient use of energy.
